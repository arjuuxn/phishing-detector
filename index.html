ℹ️ What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of online scam where attackers impersonate legitimate institutions to steal sensitive information such as login credentials, credit card numbers, or personal identity details.

🚨 How to Avoid Phishing:

  • 🔒 Never click suspicious links
  • 📧 Verify the sender's email address
  • 🌍 Use HTTPS websites only
  • ❗ Avoid urgency traps (e.g., “Act Now!”)
  • 🔍 Check for grammar/spelling mistakes
